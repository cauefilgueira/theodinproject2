Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the sauce, tossing to combine.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
