Hamburguer

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef, egg, onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into patties.
  3. Cook the patties on a grill or in a pan over medium heat for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  5. Assemble the hamburguer with the patties, lettuce, tomato, and cheese as desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
