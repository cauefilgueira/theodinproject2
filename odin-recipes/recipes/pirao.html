Brazilian Pirão

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (any white fish)
  • 1 cup of manioc flour
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cups of fish stock or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, sauté the onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the chopped tomato and cook until soft.
  3. Add the fish and cook until it flakes easily.
  4. Pour in the fish stock or water and bring to a boil.
  5. Gradually add the manioc flour, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring until the mixture thickens.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley.
